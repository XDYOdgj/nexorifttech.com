BLUEAIR Blue Pure 121 Pre-filter

Keep your home air clean and fresh with this essential accessory for the Blue Pure 121 air purifier. The washable fabric design makes maintenance simple and eco-friendly, allowing you to clean it easily with a vacuum or wash it in the sink. This pre-filter is perfect for capturing large particles like pet dander, hair, and dust bunnies, ensuring your air purifier runs efficiently and lasts longer.

With its dark shadow color, this filter blends seamlessly into most home interiors, offering both style and functionality. It's an ideal choice for anyone seeking better indoor air quality and a healthier living environment, especially those with allergies or pets.

Key Features

  • Washable fabric design for easy cleaning and reuse
  • Captures large particles like pet hair and dust
  • Dark shadow color blends with most home interiors
  • Compatible with Blue Pure 121 air purifier model
  • Improves indoor air quality by trapping airborne debris

Why Choose This Pre-filter?

By removing large contaminants before they reach the main filter, this accessory helps extend the lifespan of your air purifier and maintains optimal performance. It's a must-have for homeowners who want to simplify maintenance and enjoy cleaner, fresher air in their living spaces.
