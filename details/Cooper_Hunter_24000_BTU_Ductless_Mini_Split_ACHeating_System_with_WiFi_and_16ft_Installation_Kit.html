Cooper & Hunter Mia Series 24,000 BTU Ductless Mini Split AC/Heating System

Enjoy premium climate control with the Cooper & Hunter Mia Series 24,000 BTU Ductless Mini Split AC/Heating System. Designed for both residential and commercial use, this smart and efficient unit ensures year-round comfort no matter the season. Its high 17 SEER rating and inverter technology deliver unmatched energy savings, helping you reduce utility bills while maintaining consistent indoor temperatures.

The system’s wireless-ready smart port adapter lets you control your unit remotely from your smartphone using the C&H Remote mobile app. Access temperature settings, scheduling, and alerts anytime, anywhere. Whether you're staying cool in the summer or staying warm in the winter, the Mia Series 24,000 BTU keeps your space comfortable with its quiet Whisper Technology and powerful 24,000 BTU capacity.

This unit arrives pre-charged with refrigerant, making it easy to install and start running immediately. The 16ft installation kit includes all essential components, ensuring a smooth setup process for professional installers. With the ability to heat down to 5°F, the Cooper & Hunter Mia Series is a versatile solution for any room or commercial environment, offering reliability, efficiency, and a modern touch to your home or business.

Key Features of the Cooper & Hunter Mia Series

  • 24,000 BTU capacity for efficient cooling and heating
  • 230V system with 17 SEER and inverter technology for energy savings
  • WiFi ready with smart port adapter for remote control
  • Includes 16ft pre-flared and insulated copper line set
  • Designed for quiet operation with Whisper Technology
