Premium Craftsmanship

This decoration is meticulously handmade using high-quality colored yarn, which gives it a rich and layered appearance, almost indistinguishable from real flowers. Each petal is carefully crafted to ensure both beauty and durability.

Flexible and Adjustable

Designed with adjustable aluminum-plastic stems, this decoration allows you to shape the stems into various forms for creative and artistic setups. Its versatility makes it ideal for weddings, parties, shop displays, and more.

Durable and Low Maintenance
