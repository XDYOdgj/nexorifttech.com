AMANA K-Series PTAC Air Conditioner - Smart Comfort for Every Room

The AMANA K-Series PTAC air conditioner is a modern, efficient solution for homes. Designed with advanced Bluetooth and RF connectivity, it allows you to control your unit conveniently through the AMANA brand mobile app or a compatible thermostat. This sleek design removes the need for a traditional control panel, offering a simplified, elegant look that blends seamlessly with your interior.

Equipped with a 3.5 kW heater, this air conditioner provides both cooling and heating capabilities, making it perfect for maintaining indoor comfort through all seasons. It achieves an impressive EER rating of up to 14.0, ensuring energy efficiency and cost savings. The Quiet White color and modern finish give it a stylish appearance that complements any living space, while its noise-reduction technology ensures a peaceful, quiet environment.

Ideal for rooms up to 550 square feet, this through-the-wall AC unit is built for reliable performance and long-term value. With a STC 29 rating, it helps reduce external noise, enhancing your overall comfort. Designed and assembled in the U.S.A., it arrives fully ready for installation with a standard PTAC sleeve size, making setup straightforward and hassle-free.
