Genina Waterproof Chair Covers

Transform your dining chairs with Genina waterproof chair covers that combine practicality with a stylish, fresh look. These easy-to-install slipcovers are perfect for any home decor, offering both comfort and protection in one versatile package.

Why Choose Genina?

  • Available in a variety of colors, including classic beige, to match any interior style.
  • Waterproof fabric prevents spills and moisture from soaking into your furniture.
  • High-stretch material ensures a snug, secure fit and easy installation on most chairs.
  • Ideal for everyday use in homes, hotels, restaurants, and special events.

Perfect for Any Occasion

Whether you're hosting a dinner party, preparing for a business meeting, or simply relaxing with family, these chair covers provide the perfect blend of function and design. Their elegant jacquard pattern adds a touch of sophistication, while the slipcover's easy-clean surface makes maintenance effortless.

Versatile & Convenient

Designed for chairs with a height of 19.5-23.5 inches and back width of 16-18.5 inches, these 4-piece chair covers offer full coverage and easy storage. Add a layer of comfort and protection to your seating without sacrificing style or convenience.

Ideal for All Spaces

From dining rooms to commercial settings, Genina waterproof chair covers are a great addition to any space. They are perfect for use with Parsons chairs or other standard dining chairs, making them an essential item for both residential and professional environments.
